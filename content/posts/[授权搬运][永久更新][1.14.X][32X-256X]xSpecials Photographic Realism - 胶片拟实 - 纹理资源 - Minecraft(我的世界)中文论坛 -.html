xSpecials Photographic Realism - 胶片拟实

作者原文：
  Welcome to my texture pack.
  This is a photorealistic texture pack meaning that the purpose of it is to reflect the real life view in Minecraft. Every block is created by a photo taken of the material it represents them edited in Adobe Photoshop and fixed several parameters. As you can understand this takes a lot of time and sometimes severe Photoshop skills as there are not all blocks in nature. This texture pack is currently in progress and i am working hard on it to keep it updated and with every part of it edited.

译文：
  欢迎查看胶片拟实材质介绍。
  这是一个为了将现实视角展现在Minecraft中而存在的“超写实”材质包，每一个方块都是由经过Photoshop修改的现实材料照片所创建的，所以花费大量的时间在完成胶片拟实上也不足为奇，同时因为一些方块在现实中并不存在，有些时候你还需要高超的Photoshop技巧去刻画材质。胶片拟实仍未完成，我仍在坚持更新这款材质包，将这款材质包的所有细节刻画完成是我的第一任务。
