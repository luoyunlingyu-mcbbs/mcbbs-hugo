如何打造烟花汇演
建造烟火的演出
在新的一年里，看到令人眼花缭乱的烟花表演！
在炫丽烟火的演出中迎接新年！

克里斯·普里斯特曼

年底对我来说很难过。每个人都在看烟花在天空中绽放来庆祝新年，而我在哪里？蜷缩在桌子底下。我只是无法处理噪音：首先是烟花冲天时发出的嘶嘶声，然后......最终。。。还没有。。。近。。。砰！这不会让你想起某个绿色暴徒吗？
我的年关十分艰难。身旁每个人都在观看飞向天空的烟火，可是我呢？蜷缩在桌子底下。我只是顶不住这些声音：烟花飞行的嘶嘶声，然后...最终会...还没来...要来了...这声音是否让你想起某个绿色的怪物？

够痛苦的了。我被告知，如果我要克服恐惧，我必须面对它们。因此，对于这个新年，我不仅要尝试在众目睽睽之下观看烟花汇演，我还将举办自己的烟花表演！
十分痛苦。有人跟我说，我们遇到什么困难也不要怕，微笑着面对它！消除恐惧的最好办法就是面对恐惧！所以这个新年，我不但要完整的看一个烟火演出，还要自己办一个！

不过，我不是一个人在做——我显然没有那么勇敢。我聚集了一些 Minecraft 玩家，他们是制作盛大烟花表演的专家。他们将向我们展示它是如何完成的。我们将介绍从制作烟花到配置红石时钟到完美计时每个 BOOM 的所有内容。
但我不打算一个人弄完就是了 —— 我还没有那么勇。我召集了一些擅长巨大烟花演出的 Minecraft 玩家，他们会告诉我们如何做到这一点，从合成烟花到调整红石来踩上每个嘭的节拍。

制作烟花 合成烟花 你需要烟花才能放烟花


。听说他们很重要。所以你首先需要做的是制作一些。这是相当简单的，但是你可以创建各种形状和颜色。从哪里开始？
烟花演出首先需要烟花，听说它们很重要。所以现在你需要合成一些。不错，这很简单，但是你可以合成各种形状和颜色的，所有从哪里开始呢？

哦，是的，基础知识：烟花星星和火箭。您需要知道如何制作这两者才能在 Minecraft 中创建基本的烟花。这是你如何做到的。
欧耶，基础：烟火之星和烟花火箭。你需要知道怎么合成这两个东西，如下。

Firework star - combine gunpowder and a colour dye of your choice.
烟火之星 —— 火药和任意一个染料的组合


Firework rocket - combine gunpowder and paper.
烟花火箭 —— 火药和纸


Fireworks - combine a firework star with gunpowder and paper.
烟花 —— 烟火之星和火药与纸


Place your firework on the ground and it’ll shoot up and explode in the colour of the dye you put in it.
将烟花放在地上，它就会飞上天空，爆炸成你放入染料的颜色

Simple enough. Note that you can add more gunpowder - up to three - to make the rocket fly higher before it explodes. You can also add more dyes when creating a firework star to have it burst with more than one colour. In fact, you can have up to eight colours in a single firework star. You can make your own rainbows!
十分简单。你可以加入更多的火药 —— 最多三个 —— 让烟花在爆炸前飞的更高。更多的染料也可以让爆炸出现更多的颜色。实际上，一个烟火之星里最多可以有八种颜色。你可以搭造彩虹了！

RAINBOWS!?!?
彩虹！？！？

Yes, rainbows.
是的，彩虹。

But that’s not all. To take it further, you can add extra items to the firework recipe. Do this when crafting a firework star and your rockets will have a spectacular variety of different effects when popping in the sky.
但是不止于此，我们更进一步。你可以在烟火的配方中加入更多物品。在合成烟火之星时这样做，最终的烟花火箭在天空绽放就会有不同样式的特效。

Add fire charge to a firework star for a larger explosion. Step back unless you want a singed fringe.
增加火力就会有更大的爆炸。往后稍稍，除非你想燎着刘海。


A feather gives a burst effect to fireworks. Everyone knows that. Didn’t you pay attention in science class?
众所周知，羽毛可以让烟花呈现爆裂的效果。你上科学课没听讲吗？


Sprinkle in some glowstone dust and your firework will sparkle. That one actually makes sense.
洒些萤石粉，烟花就会闪烁起来。这个还有点道理。


Who was the rich maniac who first put a gold nugget into a firework? No matter. This is who you get a star-shaped explosion.
是哪个富人疯子先往烟花里放金粒呢？这都无所谓，但是星形的爆炸就这么出现了。


Diamonds are forever unless you put them inside a rocket destined to blow up. The trails effect is spectacular, at least.
钻石恒久远，除非你把它放在烟花里炸掉。至少尾迹特效很壮观，至少。


Show one of the decorative heads, any head, into a firework and it’ll make a Creeper-shaped firework. Great. Now I hate the noise AND how they look.
往烟火之星里加上一个装饰性的头，任何一个头，烟花就会爆炸成苦力怕形状。很棒，现在除了声音我还讨厌烟花的形状了。

Redstone Devices
红石设备

Right, you’ve got your fireworks. Now it’s time for a display! Oh, hold on, apparently just putting fireworks on the ground and watching them instantly take off isn’t a display. That’s according to the experts who say that’s just dangerous. Well I was having fun, at least.
好，你弄到烟花了，是时候演出了！噢，等等，只是把烟花放在地上看它们立刻起飞显然不算演出。专家说这样很危险。至少我乐在其中，至少。

Like there’s a safe way to handle a device that’s literally designed to explode anyway! Wait, there is? According to YouTuber stormfrenzy, we can build “sequentially connected redstone circuits” that will send the fireworks into the air for us while we stand back. That sounds... very complicated.
就好像有安全的方法处理本来设计成用来爆炸的东西一样！等等，真有？据 YouTuber stormfrenzy，我们可以建造“串行连接的红石电路”，在我们退后之后将烟花送上天。这听起来... 十分复杂。

So let’s start with the basics again. The images below demonstrate how to form simple redstone devices that let you launch fireworks from a distance (the boring way, if you ask me).
所以我们从基础开始。下图演示了如何造出在一定距离外发射烟花火箭的设备（问我就是无聊）。

Get some distance between you and the explosion with a button, a trail of redstone, and a dispenser for your fireworks.
在你和爆炸的距离外放一个按钮，一条红石线，和一个为烟花准备的发射器。


With more dispensers you can set off more fireworks at once. Add redstone repeaters and set them to different ticks to stagger their launch.
更多的发射器可以同时发射更多的烟花，不同刻的红石中继器可以错开发射的时机。


Try out different redstone repeater setups to create a range of explosive patterns.
试试不同的中继器摆法，获得不同的爆炸图案。


Make a redstone clock to have fireworks go off on a timer. Use any block at the corners and set it off by pulling the lever on and off really quickly, just once.
造一个定时发射烟花的红石钟。在转角处放上任何方块，快速拉开再关上拉杆来启动它，一次就够了。

If you can get the hang of those basics then you can start playing around with different formations to see how it affects the show in the sky. Especially important is to vary up the timing of fireworks to create contrasting moments of quiet and busyness. Also consider how long you want the display to go on for, as well as the type, colour, and height of each firework you send into the air.
如果你掌握了这些基础知识，那么接下来就可以尝试不同的组合如何影响天空中的效果。尤其重要的一点是正确的调时以创造出空闲和热烈时刻的对比。也多考虑这场演出持续的时间，以及送上天的每个烟花的种类、颜色和高度。

Minecraft tutorial website and YouTube channel Digminecraft has a good demonstration of how you can make a large redstone circuit to create a fireworks display that can last several minutes with a variety of patterns.
Minecraft 教程网站和 YouTube 频道 Digminecraft 有一个演示，你可以在那里学到如何建造巨大的红石电路，制作持续数分钟的不同图案的烟火演出。
[media]https://www.bilibili.com/video/av81392601/[/media]

Advanced Displays
高级演出

If you have an advanced understanding of how to use redstone devices then you can take your circuit much further. Stormfrenzy informs me that he connects a redstone comparator to a dispenser within a circuit to detect when the dispenser is empty of fireworks.
如果你对红石有更深的见解，那么你可以更进一步。Stormfrenzy 提醒我，他将红石比较器连接在发射器的电路上，来检测发射器何时发射完所有的烟花。

He then uses “the inverse of the redstone signal from this comparator to start powering the next redstone circuit and so on,” he tells me. “If you join a number of these redstone circuits together you can come up with some interesting fireworks displays.”
他接着使用“这个比较器的相反红石信号为下一个红石电路充能，”他告诉我。“如果你将许多这样的电路组合起来，就可以弄出一些有意思的烟火演出。”
[media]https://www.bilibili.com/video/av81381257/[/media]

Need some more inspiration for your fireworks display? Take a look at maps that showcase how other players have put together their complex shows. This one titled The Most Amazing Firework Show EVER!! by Minecraft Forum user ninjanoah5 lives up to its title.
需要为你的烟火大会准备更多灵感吗？看看这些地图里其他玩家是如何组合复杂的演出的。这个题为 有史以来最令人惊叹的烟花秀 的地图由 Minecraft 论坛用户 ninjanoah5 带来，实在不辜负其标题。

We can thank YouTuber ForgeLogical for downloading it and uploading a video of it in action. Check it out below! Yes, that is a horse flying across the sky.
我们可以感谢 YouTuber ForgeLogical 为我们下载这个地图并上传了它的视频。是的，有一只马飞过了天空。
[media]https://www.bilibili.com/video/av81387796/[/media]

Get crafting and have a great new year!
现在开始建造吧，新年快乐！
