NEWS
MINECRAFT LEGENDS OFFICIAL RELEASE DATE
《我的世界：传奇》正式发布日期

Coming to multiple platforms on April 18
4 月 18 日全平台发布


Sometimes, anticipation can be really sweet. Like going to bed before vacation and dreaming of future adventures. But the anticipation needs an end date, otherwise it’s just... “waiting.” If you’re excited to play Minecraft Legends, our upcoming action-strategy game, you’re about to find out when you can stop anticipating and start playing!
有时候，期待真是一件美好的事情，就如假期前安枕而卧，梦见未来的旅途。不过期待总得有个头，不然就只能停留在“等待”了。如果你已经在盼着畅玩我们的动作策略游戏新作《我的世界：传奇》，那么现在就能知道何时能够如愿了！

Minecraft Legends officially releases on Tuesday, April 18! That’s the day you’ll become the hero that bravely fights back against the piglins’ Nether corruption. You'll explore a vivid open world filled with treasures and perils, turn old enemies into new friends, meet new mysterious characters, and strategize with your allies in epic battles against the vicious piglins. Minecraft Legends will be available on Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 5|4, Steam, Windows 11|10, PC Game Pass, Xbox Game Pass, Xbox Series X|S, Xbox One, and Xbox Cloud Gaming.
《我的世界：传奇》将于 4 月 18 日（星期二）正式发布！到那天，你就可以化身英雄，英勇驱散猪灵带来的下界腐坏气息。你将要探索一个生机勃勃的开放世界，那里满是宝藏却也充满危险。让旧敌变作新友，去遇见神秘角色，与盟友制定策略，在可歌可泣的战斗中击退穷凶极恶的猪灵。《我的世界：传奇》将在 Nintendo Switch、PlayStation 5|4、Steam、Windows 11|10、PC 版 Game Pass、Xbox Game Pass、Xbox Series X|S、Xbox One 及 Xbox 云游戏上推出。

In less than three months, you’ll be able to gallop into the Overworld of Minecraft Legends and finally show the piglins that their rampage was a huge mistake! But first, you need to make a simple choice...
不出三个月，你就将驰骋于《我的世界：传奇》的主世界，把猪灵狠狠地教训一顿，让它们知道贸然入侵主世界是天大的失策！不过在此之前，你还需要做一点简单的选择⋯⋯


THE DELUXE EDITION AND SKIN PACK
豪华版与皮肤包

At launch, Minecraft Legends will be available in two versions: the Standard Edition (i.e. the base game) for $39.99 USD and the Deluxe Edition for $49.99 USD, which includes the base game and the Deluxe Skin pack for a discounted price!
《我的世界：传奇》将同时发售两个版本，标准版（即游戏本体）售价 39.99 美元（Steam 国区售价 119 元人民币），豪华版（包含游戏本体及优惠的豪华皮肤包）售价 49.99 美元（Steam 国区售价 139 元人民币）。



In the Deluxe Skin Pack, you’ll get one hero skin, four mount skins to change the appearance of your trusty steed, and an additional mount skin when it becomes available post launch. The Deluxe Skin Pack will be available for purchase separately down the line for $14.99 USD, so don’t worry if you don’t get the Deluxe Edition at launch. Just note that the Deluxe Edition does offer savings compared to purchasing the base game and the Deluxe Skin Pack separately.
豪华皮肤包中包含一份英雄皮肤以及四份坐骑皮肤，坐骑皮肤可以用来更改你心爱坐骑的外观，以后还会另外加入一份坐骑皮肤。豪华皮肤包可单独购买，售价 14.99 美元（约合人民币 100 元，国区实际售价待定），所以不必担心一开始没有购买豪华版。不过要注意，直接购买豪华版会比之后单独购买豪华皮肤包更加划算。

Over the coming months, we’ll be helping you prepare for the fight for the Overworld here on Minecraft.net. Check back to discover the different piglins you’ll face, the tools you’ll have at your disposal, and how your hero’s abilities can evolve throughout the campaign, among other things. Plus, keep an eye on @legends_game for the latest updates!
接下来的时间里，我们会在你正浏览的 Minecraft.net 上助你为主世界之战做足准备。稍后请回来认识一下你将要面对的各类猪灵、可以支配的各类工具、在战役中进化英雄能力的方法，还有更多信息。另外，也请关注 @legends_game，掌握最新消息。

As the sweet anticipation ends and Minecraft Legends launches on April 18, get ready to bring your courage and cunning and raise your banner high to unite the Overworld, bringing together former foes and new friends!
美好的等待将在 4 月 18 日走到终站，准备好鼓足勇气、作足巧思，高举团结主世界的大旗，让昔日的仇敌与新交的好友联合起来吧！
