官方介绍 / Official Instruction
    Soartex是Minecraft的现代64x艺术风格。最初由Soar49制作。在过去三年中由粉丝开发和更新。这些纹理经过精心设计，非常细致，但不是照片般逼真的; 也不是卡通的。Soartex有两个官方版本：Fanver和Invictus。 Fanver是Soart49制作的一个纹理包Soartex。同时我们鼓励粉丝通过论坛提供纹理。这些贡献由平面设计师审查，他们提出建议并最终确定更新的内容。Fanver以忠实于Soar49的原创作品而闻名，尽可能少的改动。 Invictus是Shoeboxam与Goldbattle共同开发的第二个特殊版本。重点转移到模式混叠，减少更大的构建，自带mipmapping优化，叶子颜色深度的预饱和度，内置着色器和块模型，全面的worldgen CTM覆盖以及与Soar49的原始艺术品的细微偏离，以进行风格复兴。Invictus可以在Github上可以看到开发版本 ，我们不接受公开赞助。
原英文介绍
Soartex is a smooth contemporary 64x art style for Minecraft. The textures, originally made by Soar49, have been developed and updated by fans over the past three years. These textures are carefully designed to be detailed, but not photo-realistic; organic, but not cartoony. There are two official continuations of Soartex: Fanver and Invictus.   Fanver is a community continuation of Soartex, a texture pack by Soar49. Fans are encouraged to contribute textures through the forum. These contributions are reviewed by graphic designers, who make friendly recommendations and ultimately determine the content of updates. Fanver is known for being faithful to the original work of Soar49, with as few alterations as possible.   Invictus is a second continuation developed by Shoeboxam with Goldbattle. The focus shifts to pattern and aliasing reduction for bigger builds, mipmapping optimization, presaturation for color depth in foliage, built in shaders and block models, comprehensive worldgen CTM overrides and a subtle departure from Soar49's original artwork for a stylistic revival. Development is visible on the Invictus Github, but public contributions are not being accepted.
