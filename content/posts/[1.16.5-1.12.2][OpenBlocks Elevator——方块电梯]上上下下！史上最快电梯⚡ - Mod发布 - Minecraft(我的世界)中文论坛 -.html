简介 （作者：vsngarcia ） （此排版来自PCD小组）
OpenBlocks Elevator 是一款轻量级而且十分方便好用的电梯mod，使用起来也十分顺畅。（使用效果有点像是使用TP指令一样瞬移）
OpenBlocks Elevator 支持玩家在垂直的两个电梯方块上进行上下传送（站在电梯方块上，按跳跃就上升一层楼，按蹲就下降一层）
OpenBlocks Elevator 其实是OpenBlocks（开放式方块）下的一个物品，现在成为了一个独立的模组。

作者简介：

Simple port of the elevator from OpenBlocks to 1.8+ (Forge) Crafting is the same.
There can be blocks between them, they just have to be on the same X and Z.
Stand on the block and jump to go up and crouch to go down.
When directional is enabled, it adjusts your camera when teleporting to it.
You can hide the arrow in the GUI. Camouflage elevators by simply right clicking with any block.

配置教程
配置文件位于 /config/elevatorid-server.toml
以下配置翻译大多为机翻，希望大家能提出更好更合适的翻译，谢谢。

#Should elevators have the same color in order to teleport ?
＃电梯应该具有相同的颜色才能进行传送吗？
sameColor = false

#Realign players to the center of elevator ?
＃传送时将玩家调整到电梯中央？
precisionTarget = true

#Elevator range
#Range: 3 ~ 256
range = 256

#Reset pitch to 0 when teleporting to directional elevators ?
resetPitchDirectional = true

#Reset pitch to 0 when teleporting to normal elevators ?
resetPitchNormal = false

#Can mobs spawn on elevators ?
#电梯上是否刷怪
mobSpawn = false

#Should teleporting require XP ?
＃传送需要消耗XP吗？
useXP = false

#Amount of XP points to use when
#当使用电梯时消耗的XP点数
useXP is enabled

#Note this is NOT experience levels
#Range: > 1
XPPointsAmount = 1
