简介
村庄多元化Mod增加了更多的村庄和更多的遗迹。
我们的目标是让你在你的世界中探索，和发现令人惊奇的新东西。
你会发现新的村民，与新的村民进行交易；你会发现新的怪物，这可能是非常危险的。
有了这个Mod，你就可以和你的朋友们拥有一段好时光,生活在一些史诗般的故事中！

THE DIVERSITY WIP MOD
WIP Thread - Suggestion Thread

Hello, I'm Budd, a French modder. Here is my last mod, named Diversity.
The Diversity Mod adds more villages and more structures ingame. Our aim is to make you explore and find surprising new stuff in your world. You will find new villagers, with new trades, you will find new monsters which can be very dangerous.
With that tool, you will be able to have good time with your friends and live some epic stories !
Share and help us to make it evolve as best as possible !
